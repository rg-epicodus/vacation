Staying in the Yurt

We stayed in a yurt for the trip. The cost was low so we got to splurge on other things like:

  • A visit to the spa for mom and dad
  • A kite to fly at the beach in the wind
  • Some souvenirs at the aquarium
  • A nice dinner when we got home

The Journey Begins

First we got in the car and then after going to the gas station, we filled up the gas tank. It took about 3 hours to drive to the aquarium, the weather was sunny and warm. It was a beautiful day. Once we got into Newport we stopped by Rogue Brewery for lunch. We had some beer first and got the kids Root Bear. I had an appetizer of fries with some melted chees on them and then a Kobe Beef Hamburger. Aimee got a Salmon fillet. We were not really excited about the food, we cook better at home, but it was nice not having to worry about washing a bunch of dishes afterward.
